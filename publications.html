On 2013-2014 I worked under the Tracker Group of Compact Muon Solenoid (CMS) experiment in Physics Department at Cern.I was involved mainly in two projects. The first one has to do with the design and the monitor of the control system of the new cooling plant installed for the Tracker Detector of CMS experiment using PLC systems. The second one was the preparation, calibration, installation and development of a network of dew-point (silicon and fiber optic) sensors targeted to online monitoring at critical areas of the CMS Tracker sealing in view of the post Long Shutdown. Technical reports are published below.

  • Tracker going cold , Technical Documentation for the control implementation and monitoring of the new cooling plant for the Tracker Detector at Cern.


  • Control code of the Arduino system at CMS , Technical Documentation for the temporary sensor network installed at Cern.
